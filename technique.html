Techniques

There are 4 swimming techniques, which are called strokes: Butterfly, breaststroke, Backstroke and Freestlyse. The combination of all 4 strokes is called Medley. The stroke I chose for myself is butterfly. In all competitions I would perform that stroke. To get to know more about the right way of swimming watch a video or read an article (link in footage)
